Branch 1 (Colombo City Centre)

Colombo City Centre: A comprehensive healthcare facility offering a wide range of primary care services. Our dedicated team provides routine check-ups, preventive care, and general medical consultations for patients of all ages in the heart of Colombo.

Branch 2 (Colombo Townhall)

Colombo Townhall: Our advanced medical center focusing on specialized care. Equipped with cutting-edge technology and staffed by expert specialists, this branch offers advanced treatments in areas such as cardiology, neurology, and oncology

Branch 3 (Kalutara)

Kalutara: A serene healing environment dedicated to patient recovery and rehabilitation. This branch provides comprehensive physical therapy, occupational therapy, and long-term care programs, helping patients regain independence and improve their quality of life.
